Projects

PHOEBE (Placing Headgear Optodes Efficiently Before Experiment)

lpollonini/phoebe

PHOEBE is a MATLAB GUI application that measures and displays the optical coupling between fNIRS optodes and the scalp of a subject in real-time. The primary goal is to help experimenters optimize signal quality of fNIRS headgear efficiently prior to recordings.

Key Contributions:

  • Developed quantitative measures of scalp coupling for individual optodes, similar to electrical skin conductance used in EEG.
  • Created intuitive visualization of scalp coupling status on a head model to guide quick adjustments.
  • Implemented quality assessment for individual optical channels (source-detector pairings).
  • Addressed critical challenges in fNIRS setup time and signal quality that particularly affect pediatric and elderly populations.

Technical Details:

  • Collaborated on metrics for quality assessment including Scalp Coupling Index and Peak Spectral Power.
  • Achieved optimal trade-off between lengthy preparation time and signal quality.
  • Developed interface accessible to non-technical personnel.
  • Integrated with sister project QT-NIRS for post-recording quality assessment.
